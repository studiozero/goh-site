Get Over Here

Get over here is a web-based application made to help you get the attention of someone, anyone. A friend, A stranger, A drummer or even a taxi driver. It was inspired by Lookfor but is simpler, free, Open Source and accessed via the web so it can work on your smartphone, your tablet, your laptop, your Windows phone or any device that has access to a web browser. You can even save the app to your homescreen for use later on and anytime offline. Read about its creation here and then access it below.
